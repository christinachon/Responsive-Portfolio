About Me


Hey! My name is Christina Chon and this is my website.

I'm looking forward to learning more about web and software development. I'm originally from California and moved to Seattle about 10 years ago (wow, time flies!) I attended school at University of California, Riverside and recieved my Bachelor's of Arts in Psychology.

My past job experience is working with children in the childcare setting and as a behavior therapist. Currently, I am working within University Recruiting with Microsoft and seeing all the new college graduates starting their journey in the workforce. Seeing all the new Software Engineers encouraged me to take a chance and learn how to code. It was something that always looked interesting and if they can do it, why can't I?

My other hobbies that I enjoy are indoor bouldering, playing video games (Witcher 3 and Zelda BOTW are my current favorites!), and acting. I have one small dog that I love to play with and take on walks around the neighborhood or take to the park.
